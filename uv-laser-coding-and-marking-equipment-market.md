<p><p>UV laser coding and marking equipment offers distinct types of marking techniques tailored for diverse applications. Mask marking involves using a stencil or mask to selectively expose areas for permanent marking, ensuring precision. Array marking enables high-speed marking across multiple points simultaneously, ideal for larger production runs. Scanning marking employs a movable laser to create detailed designs or text with high accuracy on various surfaces, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in coding processes. Each method optimizes quality and adaptability in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
