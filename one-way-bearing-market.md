<p><p>One-way bearing markets consist of various designs catering to specific applications. The wedge design features a mechanism that allows rotation in one direction while preventing reverse motion, ideal for applications requiring secure locking. The self-locking angle design employs angled surfaces to achieve a tight grip, ensuring stability under high loads. The slope design incorporates inclined surfaces for smooth engagement and disengagement, while the roller design utilizes rollers to reduce friction, enhancing efficiency and durability in rotational applications.</p></p>
