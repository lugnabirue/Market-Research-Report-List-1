<p><p>The auto dash camera market is primarily segmented into two types: single-channel and multi-channel cameras. Single-channel auto dash cameras capture video from one angle, typically focused on the road ahead, providing essential evidence in case of incidents. In contrast, multi-channel auto dash cameras feature multiple lenses to capture footage from various angles, including the front, rear, and sides of the vehicle. This multi-perspective approach enhances security and safety by offering comprehensive coverage of the surroundings while driving.</p></p>
