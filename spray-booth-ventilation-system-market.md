<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Spray Booth Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Draft Spray Booth Ventilation System</li><li>Cross Draft Spray Booth Ventilation System</li><li>Down Draft Spray Booth Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spray Booth Ventilation System market encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **Side Draft Spray Booth** - Airflow moves horizontally from one side to another, effectively capturing overspray while maintaining uniform ventilation.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Cross Draft Spray Booth** - Air circulates across the workspace from one side to the other, providing fast drying times but may have uneven airflow.</p><p>3. **Down Draft Spray Booth** - Air is drawn vertically from above the work area, enhancing overspray removal and providing a clean environment, ideal for intricate painting tasks. Each type serves specific applications and efficiency needs.</p></p>
