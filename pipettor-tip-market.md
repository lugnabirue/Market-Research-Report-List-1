<p><p>The pipettor tip market is categorized mainly into non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for routine laboratory applications, allowing for accurate liquid transfer without special contamination prevention features. In contrast, filtered pipette tips include a built-in barrier that prevents aerosols and contaminants from entering the pipette, making them ideal for applications involving volatile, hazardous, or biological samples. Both types cater to varying laboratory needs, ensuring precision and reliability in liquid handling.</p></p>
