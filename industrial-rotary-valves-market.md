<p><p>The industrial rotary valves market consists of several key types, including butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves. Butterfly valves are characterized by a rotating disc that regulates flow, offering quick operation and compact design. Ball valves utilize a spherical closure element that provides tight sealing and durability, suitable for on-off applications. Plug valves employ a cylindrical or tapered plug to control flow, allowing for smooth and reliable operation. Each type serves specific industrial needs, ensuring efficient flow control in various processes.</p></p>
