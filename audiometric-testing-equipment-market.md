<p><ul><li>Screening Audiometry</li><li>Diagnostic Audiometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audiometric testing equipment market is categorized into two primary types: screening audiometry and diagnostic audiometry. Screening audiometry focuses on quickly identifying individuals who may have hearing impairments, often conducted in schools or primary care settings. It utilizes portable devices for efficient testing. In contrast, diagnostic audiometry provides a comprehensive evaluation of hearing function, employing sophisticated equipment to determine the type and degree of hearing loss. This is typically performed in specialized clinics or hospitals for in-depth analysis and tailored treatment.</p></p>
