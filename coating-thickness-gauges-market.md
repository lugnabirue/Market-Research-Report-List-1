<p><ul><li>Eddy Current Technology</li><li>Ultrasonic Technology</li><li>X-Ray Technology</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coating thickness gauges are categorized based on measurement technology. Eddy current technology utilizes electromagnetic induction to measure non-magnetic coatings on conductive substrates. Ultrasonic technology employs sound waves to assess thickness, effective for various materials. X-ray technology, a non-destructive method, analyzes the absorption of X-rays to determine coating thickness, especially useful for multi-layer coatings. Other market types may include mechanical and optical methods, offering diverse solutions based on specific application requirements and material characteristics. Each technology presents unique advantages for precise measurement.</p></p>
