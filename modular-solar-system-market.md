<p><strong>The Modular Solar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tie Solar</li><li>Off-Grid Solar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular solar system market comprises two primary types: grid-tie solar and off-grid solar. Grid-tie solar systems are connected to the utility grid, allowing users to sell excess energy back to providers, benefiting from reduced energy costs and incentives. In contrast, off-grid solar systems operate independently from the grid, utilizing battery storage to supply power in remote areas. This setup offers energy autonomy and reliability, especially in locations lacking access to conventional electricity infrastructure.</p></p>
