<p><strong>The Retread Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre Cure</li><li>Mold Cure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The retread tire market is primarily segmented into two types: pre-cure and mold cure. Pre-cure retreads involve applying a pre-cured tread rubber onto a prepared casing, providing flexibility in tread design and faster production. In contrast, mold cure retreads utilize a tread that is cured in a mold, leading to a more uniform finish and typically superior performance but with longer production times. Both methods aim to extend the life of tires while promoting sustainability in the automotive industry.</p></p>
