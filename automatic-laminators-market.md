<p><ul><li>Two Side Laminators</li><li>One Side Laminators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic laminators market is categorized into two main types: one side laminators and two side laminators. One side laminators are designed to apply a protective laminate only to one side of the substrate, making them ideal for applications where only front-side protection is needed. In contrast, two side laminators apply laminate to both sides, providing enhanced durability and protection, suitable for items like ID cards or menus. Each type serves distinct needs based on application requirements and end-user preferences.</p></p>
