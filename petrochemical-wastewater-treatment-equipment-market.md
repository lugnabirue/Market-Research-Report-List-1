<p><p>The petrochemical wastewater treatment equipment market consists of various treatment types. Biological treatment utilizes microorganisms to decompose organic pollutants. Ion exchange treatment employs charged resins to remove dissolved ions, effectively reducing contaminant levels. Membrane treatment uses semi-permeable membranes to separate pollutants from water, offering high efficiency. Physico-chemical treatment combines physical and chemical processes to remove particulates and dissolved substances, addressing a wide range of contaminants. Each method targets specific pollutants, ensuring safer disposal and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
