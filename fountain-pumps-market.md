<p><strong>The Fountain Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible Type</li><li>In-line Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fountain pumps market is primarily categorized into two types: submersible and in-line pumps. Submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, providing efficient water movement for ponds, fountains, and aquariums. They are characterized by their compact design and quiet operation. In-line pumps, on the other hand, are installed above water level and are typically used for larger water features, allowing for higher flow rates and easier maintenance. Both types cater to different applications and user preferences in water management solutions.</p></p>
