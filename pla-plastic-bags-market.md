<p><p>PLA plastic bags are primarily derived from renewable sources, primarily focusing on two types: corn starch extraction and sugarcane extraction. Corn starch extraction utilizes starch from corn to produce polylactic acid (PLA), which is then formed into biodegradable bags. In contrast, sugarcane extraction employs sugarcane as the base material, leveraging its high sugar content to create an eco-friendly alternative. Both methods prioritize sustainability, contributing to decreasing reliance on fossil fuels, while offering biodegradable options that reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
