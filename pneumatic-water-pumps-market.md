<p><strong>The Pneumatic Water Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Submersible</li><li>Dry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic water pumps are categorized primarily into submersible and dry types. Submersible pumps operate underwater, utilizing a sealed motor to effectively move water from deep sources, ideal for wells and drainage applications. In contrast, dry pumps are designed to work above the water level and rely on suction to draw water into the system. Each type serves distinct needs based on the operational environment and specific application requirements, with submersible being suitable for submerged environments and dry pumps for above-ground water handling.</p></p>
