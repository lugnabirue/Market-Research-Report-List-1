<p><p>Shoulder Elbow Wrist Hand Orthosis (SEWHO) devices are specialized supports designed to stabilize and assist the upper extremities. The market for SEWHO devices is divided into two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to facilitate movement while providing support, often incorporating adjustable components that allow for functional use during rehabilitation. In contrast, static orthotics immobilize the joints to promote healing, reduce pain, or correct deformities, making them essential for post-surgical recovery and injury management.</p></p>
