<p><p>The organic oats market comprises several types, each catering to specific consumer preferences. Steel cut oats are whole oat groats chopped into pieces, offering a chewy texture and nutty flavor. Rolled oats are steamed and flattened, making them quick to prepare and versatile for baking. Oat flour is finely ground oats, often used as a gluten-free alternative in baking. Oats bran, the outer layer of the oat groat, is high in fiber and often added to cereals and health foods for its nutritional benefits.</p></p>
