<p><p>The Armored Unmanned Underwater Vehicle (UUV) market primarily features two types: Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs) and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs). ROVs are tethered to an operator on the surface, allowing real-time control for tasks like inspection and maintenance in hazardous environments. In contrast, AUVs operate independently, utilizing pre-programmed missions for tasks such as seabed mapping and environmental monitoring. Both types are essential for defense, exploration, and research, enhancing underwater capabilities while mitigating risks to human divers.</p></p>
