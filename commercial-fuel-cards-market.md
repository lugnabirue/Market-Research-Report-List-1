<p><p>The commercial fuel cards market is segmented into three main types: magnetic stripe cards, optical cards, and chip cards. Magnetic stripe cards contain a magnetic strip that stores data, allowing quick access and transaction processing. Optical cards use visual encoding techniques, such as barcodes, for information retrieval, offering a simple yet effective security measure. Chip cards, featuring embedded microchips, provide enhanced security and data storage capabilities, making them resistant to fraud, and are increasingly popular for secure transactions in the industry.</p></p>
