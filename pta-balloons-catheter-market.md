<p><p>PTA (Percutaneous Transluminal Angioplasty) Balloon Catheters are medical devices used in minimally invasive procedures to treat narrowed or obstructed blood vessels. They are primarily employed in the management of conditions such as peripheral artery disease (PAD), coronary artery disease (CAD), and other vascular issues.</p><p>The PTA Balloon Catheters Market is experiencing significant growth, fueled by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in catheter technology, and a rising aging population.
