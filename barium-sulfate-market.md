<p><p>The Barium Sulfate market consists of two main types: Precipitated Barium Sulfate and Ultra-Fine Precipitated Barium Sulfate. Precipitated Barium Sulfate, with a D50 particle size greater than 0.5μm, is widely used in paints, coatings, and plastics for its opacity and opacity enhancement. In contrast, Ultra-Fine Precipitated Barium Sulfate, with a D50 particle size of 0.5μm or less, offers superior performance in applications requiring finer particles, such as in pharmaceuticals and advanced coatings for improved dispersibility and transparency.</p></p>
