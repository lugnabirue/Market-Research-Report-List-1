<p><p>The Automatic Guided System (AGS) market comprises three key types: magnetic, electronic, and laser systems. Magnetic AGS utilizes magnetic tape or strips to guide vehicles, ensuring precision in navigation. Electronic systems rely on sensors and signals for real-time guidance, offering flexibility and adaptability in various environments. Laser AGS employs laser beams to provide accurate positioning and guidance, often used in complex settings like warehouses. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing efficiency and safety in automated transport operations.</p></p>
