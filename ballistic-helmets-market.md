<p><p>The ballistic helmets market is categorized into three material types: metal, nonmetal, and composite. Metal helmets, often made from steel or aluminum, offer robust protection but can be heavy and less comfortable. Nonmetal helmets typically use materials like polycarbonate or fiberglass, providing a balance of weight and protection, although they may have limitations in high-impact scenarios. Composite helmets combine materials to enhance durability, weight, and comfort, making them popular for modern military and law enforcement applications.</p></p>
