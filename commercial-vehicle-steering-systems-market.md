<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Power Steering</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering</li><li>Electro-hydraulic Power Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle steering systems market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Electronic Power Steering (EPS)** employs electric motors to assist steering, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing weight. </p><p>2. **Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)** utilizes hydraulic fluid pressure for steering assistance, providing a traditional, reliable solution with a robust feel. </p><p>3. **Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)** combines both systems, using electric motors to control hydraulic pumps, offering precise steering with improved responsiveness and adaptability to varying vehicle conditions.</p></p>
