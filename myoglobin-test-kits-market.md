<p><p>Myoglobin test kits come in various types, each utilizing different methodologies. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) kits are widely used for their sensitivity and specificity, enabling quantification of myoglobin in samples. Colorimetric kits provide a visual color change indicating myoglobin levels, ideal for quick assessments. Microplate enzyme immunoassays allow high-throughput testing, suitable for large sample volumes. Other methods may include immunochromatographic assays or digital assays, offering diverse options based on laboratory needs and testing environments.</p></p>
