<p><strong>The Fly Ash Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class F Fly Ash</li><li>Class C Fly Ash</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fly ash cement market is primarily divided into two types: Class F fly ash and Class C fly ash. Class F fly ash is derived from burning anthracite or bituminous coal and has pozzolanic properties, used to enhance strength in concrete. In contrast, Class C fly ash, produced from lignite or sub-bituminous coal, has both pozzolanic and cementitious properties, enabling it to bind independently. Both types are essential for sustainable construction, improving concrete durability and reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
