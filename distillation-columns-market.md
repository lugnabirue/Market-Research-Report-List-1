<p><p>Distillation columns are crucial in separating mixtures based on varying boiling points. The market is divided into batch columns and continuous columns. Batch columns operate in discrete cycles, ideal for small-scale production and specialty chemicals, allowing for flexibility in operation. Conversely, continuous columns function continuously, maximizing efficiency and throughput, making them suitable for large-scale operations in petrochemicals and pharmaceuticals. The choice between these types often depends on production volume, product variability, and operational requirements in the chemical industry.</p></p>
