<p><p>The Double Lumen Bronchial Tubes market is categorized into Left and Right Double Lumen Bronchial Tubes. The Left Double Lumen Bronchial Tubes are designed for optimal placement in the left mainstem bronchus, facilitating selective ventilation and one-lung anesthesia for surgical procedures involving the right lung. Conversely, the Right Double Lumen Bronchial Tubes are tailored for the right mainstem bronchus, serving similar functions in procedures focused on the left lung. Both types are essential for enhancing surgical outcomes in thoracic surgeries.</p></p>
