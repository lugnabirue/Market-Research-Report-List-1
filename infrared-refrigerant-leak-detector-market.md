<p><p>The infrared refrigerant leak detector market is categorized into three main types: CFC Leak Detectors, HFC Leak Detectors, and HCFC Leak Detectors. CFC Leak Detectors are designed for detecting chlorofluorocarbons, commonly used in older refrigeration systems. HFC Leak Detectors target hydrofluorocarbons, which are prevalent in modern applications due to their lower ozone depletion potential. HCFC Leak Detectors focus on hydrochlorofluorocarbons, serving as transitional refrigerants. Each type utilizes infrared technology to accurately identify specific refrigerant leaks, ensuring efficient maintenance and environmental compliance.</p></p>
