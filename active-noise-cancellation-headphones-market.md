<p><p>Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) headphones are categorized into three main types: In-Ear, On-Ear, and Over-Ear. In-Ear headphones fit snugly within the ear canal, offering portability and discrete noise cancellation. On-Ear headphones rest on the outer ear, providing a balance between sound isolation and comfort, making them suitable for casual use. Over-Ear headphones envelop the entire ear, delivering superior sound quality and maximum noise isolation, ideal for immersive listening experiences. Each type caters to different user preferences and environments.</p></p>
