<p><p>The nano fibers market is categorized based on production methods: Molecular techniques involve chemical processes to create ultra-fine fibers at the molecular level, enhancing properties such as strength and surface area. Spinning methods, including electrospinning and melt spinning, utilize mechanical techniques to produce fibers by extruding polymer solutions or melts, offering versatility in fiber characteristics. Biological methods harness natural processes or organisms to synthesize fibers, promoting sustainability and eco-friendliness in production. Each method contributes uniquely to the diverse applications of nano fibers.</p></p>
