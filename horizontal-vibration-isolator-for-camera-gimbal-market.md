<p><p>The Horizontal Vibration Isolator for Camera Gimbal market is divided into two primary types: those with hydraulic dampers and those without. Isolators with hydraulic dampers employ fluid-based mechanisms to absorb and dissipate energy from vibrations, providing enhanced stability for camera systems during movement. Conversely, isolators without hydraulic dampers utilize mechanical springs or elastic materials to mitigate vibrations, typically offering a simpler and often cheaper solution. Both types aim to improve image quality by minimizing unwanted camera shake.</p></p>
